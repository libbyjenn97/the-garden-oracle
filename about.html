Garden Weather Oracle is a mystical gardening companion that combines ancient wisdom with modern seed management. It's a tool for gardeners who believe in the magic of the moon, the wisdom of the seasons, and the power of intention in growing food and flowers.

This web application blends oracle card divination, lunar gardening principles, and practical seed organisation to help you plan and nurture your garden with both intuition and information.

üîÆ Oracle Cards

Draw from a deck of 44 beautifully illustrated oracle cards inspired by spring celebrations, natural cycles, and gardening wisdom. Each card offers:

  • Mystical Guidance: Symbolic meanings to inspire your gardening journey
  • Practical Advice: Specific planting suggestions aligned with the card's energy
  • Moon Affinity: Optimal lunar phases for the card's themes
  • Seasonal Wisdom: Best times of year to work with each card's energy

Use the cards for daily inspiration, seasonal planning, or whenever you need guidance in your garden.

üåô Lunar Gardening Calendar

Plan your planting by the moon's phases with our interactive lunar calendar. Based on traditional moon gardening principles:

  • Accurate Moon Phases: Real-time calculation of current lunar phase
  • Planting Guidance: Specific tasks recommended for each moon phase
  • Above Ground vs Root Crops: Optimal timing for different plant types
  • Monthly View: Navigate through months to plan ahead

The waxing moon (new to full) is ideal for above-ground crops, while the waning moon (full to new) favours root vegetables and garden maintenance.

üå± My Seeds

Connect your Notion seed database to view and manage your entire seed collection:

  • Complete Inventory: See all your seeds in one beautiful interface
  • Smart Filtering: Search by name, plant type, planting month, or moon phase
  • Current Conditions: Filter by today's moon phase and month
  • Detailed Information: View classification, timing, growing conditions, and status
  • Seed Selection: Choose seeds to create your planting spread

Your seed data is cached locally for fast access and syncs with your Notion database.
